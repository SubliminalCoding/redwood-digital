MeCoach

A private AI coach that lives in your pocket. Voice-led sessions that help users work through what's actually on their plate: career pressure, life goals, blockers they haven't named yet.

For teams: offer it as a benefit. Employees get a safe, always-on place to think out loud, set goals, and track action items. You get retention without running coaching programs yourself. HIPAA-aware data model; private to the individual.

VoiceForge

Desktop voice-to-text that lives in any app. Press a hotkey, speak, and the cleaned-up text pastes wherever your cursor was, under two seconds end to end.

For businesses: sales teams, field inspectors, practitioners typing notes all day. VoiceForge replaces the keyboard for anyone whose job is talking more than typing. Cross-platform, offline-capable, learns your vocabulary and technical jargon. Zero cloud dependency. The audio never leaves the machine.

  • Hotkey-triggered capture anywhere on the OS: docs, email, CRM, browser
  • Runs fully offline on the local machine; no audio sent to any server
  • Learns proper nouns, industry terms, internal abbreviations over time
  • Daily stats dashboard: words transcribed, avg WPM, transcription count
